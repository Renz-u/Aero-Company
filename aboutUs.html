Our Journey

2010: Foundation

AERO INC. was founded by Dr. Amelia Hart in a small research lab, focusing initially on renewable energy systems.

2015: First Major Launch

Successfully deployed our proprietary satellite communication network, establishing our presence in the aerospace sector.

2020: Global Expansion

Opened international R&D centers in Europe and Asia, growing our team to over 500 dedicated experts.

2024: Sustainable Initiative

Launched the 'Green Skies' project, committing to 100% carbon-neutral operations by 2030.
