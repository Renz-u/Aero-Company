Our Story

Founded in October 2025 by Laurence Anthony Geronga (CEO), Etienne Prince Lagana (CTO), and Mark Kevin Ecleo (COO), Aero Inc. has been at the forefront of spacecraft innovation and sustainable space exploration.

  • Oct 2025: Company founded
  • Jan 2026: First prototype Nova X completed
  • Feb 2026: Orion V mid-range craft launch
  • Apr 2026: Deep Space mission tests
